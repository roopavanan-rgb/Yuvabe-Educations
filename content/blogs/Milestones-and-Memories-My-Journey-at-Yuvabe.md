In this blog, Ajay Kumar takes us through his transformative journey as a Tech Analyst at Yuvabe, where his growth has extended beyond just honing technical skills. In this blog, he shares the key milestones and unforgettable memories that have shaped his path, offering insights into both his professional and personal growth.

![Ajay Kumar at Yuvabe, Auroville](/images/blogs/4c1519_893cf6708d4f477a8c3e018dc8daf6bf~mv2.png#md-left)

Hello! My name is Ajay Kumar R., and I am a tech enthusiast with a passion for solving problems! I enjoy exploring new technologies, and when I’m not coding or gaming you’ll likely find me petting dogs or exploring new cuisines. My journey so far has been shaped by an inherent curiosity and a desire to grow, both personally and professionally.

In the year 2020, I completed my Bachelors in Computer Administration from Pondicherry. Then, a little over a year ago, I joined the dynamic Yuvabe team as a Tech Analyst. Looking back, it’s been an incredible ride; the opportunity to contribute to the company’s development, alongside such talented people, has been nothing short of rewarding.

### A New Chapter at Yuvabe

Before stepping into Yuvabe, I worked as a web developer in a small team, handling both front-end and back-end development. While it helped me gain a solid foundation, I often felt like my growth was stunted. I craved challenges, deeper learning, and a more supportive, collaborative environment. This search led me to Yuvabe, where I got the chance to not only continue web development but to broaden my horizons by diving into the Frappe Framework and ERPNext for business solutions.

### Key Projects That Shaped My Growth

Since joining Yuvabe, I’ve had the privilege of working on some exciting projects. Each one has helped me grow, sharpen my skills, and build solutions that truly make a difference. Here are a few standout experiences:

![Ajay with his Yuvabe colleague at HERS, Auroville,, building an ERP system](/images/blogs/4c1519_4b3a9eeb92084917b3fd807741c77723~mv2.png#lg-center)

CEBA (Common ERPNext Backbone for Auroville): This project was aimed at improving food security in Auroville by integrating and streamlining processes across several community units. Using ERPNext, we worked on designing a unified system that fostered better collaboration and increased efficiency.

Svaram: For this project, we customised ERPNext to fit their specific business needs, connecting it to their WooCommerce platform for the seamless sale of musical instruments.

Meld Café: A hospitality-focused project, where I helped implement an ERPNext system to manage the café’s daily operations, streamlining everything from inventory to customer orders.

Lychee: This was another ERPNext-WooCommerce integration project where we delivered a solid, reliable solution for the client’s e-commerce needs.

Working on these projects has been fulfilling; each project gave me an opportunity to apply my coding skills in a meaningful way while also learning something new.

### Expanding Horizons with DevOps

In addition to honing my web development skills, I’ve also ventured into the world of DevOps, thanks to the guidance of my experienced colleagues. This has been a game-changer, allowing me to contribute to projects with a broader understanding of the entire development lifecycle. It’s pushed me to think beyond code and look at the bigger picture—how the systems we build and maintain can evolve and scale!

### Serving the Community

One of the unique aspects of working at Yuvabe is its focus on community engagement through the Work.Serve.Evolve philosophy. This led me to volunteer at Solat Kitchen, a community kitchen in Auroville, where I assisted in serving meals to residents.

![The Yuvabe team took a weekend hike to Devadana](/images/blogs/4c1519_735a5d3408b14b56b6a10db88c1baa24~mv2.png#md-right)

Another rewarding experience has been my role as a STEAM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Art, and Mathematics) facilitator at Auroville's Deepanam School. Interacting with the students has been a joyful experience, and these moments have taught me valuable lessons in communication and patience. These opportunities have added a new dimension to my learning journey, allowing me to give back to the community while developing soft skills that are just as crucial as technical ones.

### Looking Ahead

The collaborative environment at Yuvabe has not only nurtured my growth as a developer but has also inspired me to constantly strive for excellence. My journey so far is a reflection of the learning opportunities and support that Yuvabe provides, pushing each of us to push our limits and achieve more.

I’m excited for what lies ahead, knowing that every project, every challenge, and every interaction will only add more value to my personal and professional growth.
